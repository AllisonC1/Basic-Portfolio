This is a game that I created.


"Elf Hunts Orc"


The objective of this game:


The objective of this game is to have the elf shoot the orc with his arrow.


How play the game:

Press the green flag to start.

Use the Left Arrow Button on your keyboard to go Left.
Use the Right Arrow Button on your keyboard to go Right.
Use the Space Button on your keyboard to have the elf shoot an arrow at the orc.
Click the red "Stop" sign to the right of the green flag to stop the game.
If the orc touches the elf, then the game is over.
